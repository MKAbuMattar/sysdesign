---
description: Name the tradeoffs of a design choice or architecture
---

Use the `system-design` skill. Subject: `$ARGUMENTS`.

List the tradeoffs the choice makes, one per line as `<axis>: <what you gain> vs <what you give up>`. Cover only the axes that apply — consistency vs availability, strong vs eventual, latency vs throughput, cost vs redundancy, sync vs async, normalization vs denormalization, simplicity vs flexibility, build vs buy.

End with the one tradeoff that dominates for the stated constraint, and the condition that would flip the decision.
